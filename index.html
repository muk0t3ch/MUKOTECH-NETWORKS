Networking Gadgets

Comprehensive Networking Solutions

Networking gadgets are hardware devices that help connect computers, printers, servers, and other devices to a network, enabling communication, data transfer, and internet access.

Common Types:
  • Routers
  • Switches
  • Modems
  • Network Interface Cards (NICs)
  • Wireless Access Points
  • Network Attached Storage (NAS)

We provide complete installation, configuration, and maintenance services for all types of networking equipment.

CCTV Installation

Professional CCTV Solutions

CCTV (Closed-Circuit Television) installation involves setting up a surveillance system to monitor and record activities in a specific area.

Installation Process:
  1. Site Survey and Planning
  2. Camera Selection (Dome, Bullet, PTZ, etc.)
  3. DVR/NVR Setup
  4. Cable Routing and Connection
  5. System Configuration
  6. Testing and Demonstration

Our team provides end-to-end CCTV solutions for homes, businesses, and industrial facilities.
