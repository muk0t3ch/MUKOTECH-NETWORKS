Warranty Policy

Our commitment to quality service and products

Service Warranty

  • 30 Days All labor services come with a 30-day warranty unless otherwise specified.
  • 90 Days Hardware repairs and installations have a 90-day warranty.
  • 1 Year Network installations come with a 1-year warranty on workmanship.

Coverage: Our service warranty covers defects in workmanship and ensures that services are performed to professional standards. It does not cover:

  • Issues caused by client misuse or unauthorized modifications
  • Problems resulting from external factors (power surges, natural disasters, etc.)
  • Normal wear and tear

Product Warranty

Products sold by MUKOTECH NETWORKS come with the manufacturer's warranty. Typical warranty periods:

Product Category Warranty Period Notes
New Hardware 1-3 years Varies by manufacturer
Refurbished Equipment 6-12 months Depends on supplier terms
Software As per license Typically 1 year
Peripherals 1 year Some items may have less

Claim Process: To claim warranty service:

  1. Contact us with proof of purchase
  2. Provide details of the issue
  3. We will assess and advise on next steps
  4. For manufacturer warranties, we can facilitate the process

Warranty Exclusions

The following void all warranties:

  • Unauthorized repairs or modifications
  • Physical damage from accidents or misuse
  • Exposure to liquids, extreme temperatures, or other environmental factors
  • Use with incompatible hardware/software
  • Removal or alteration of manufacturer labels/serial numbers

Refund Policy

Our guidelines for returns and refunds

General Refund Principles

  • 7 Days Unopened hardware may be returned within 7 days for full refund.
  • 30 Days Defective products may be returned within 30 days for replacement or refund.
  • N/A Services are generally non-refundable once rendered.
  • Case-by-Case Custom software and digital products may have special refund conditions.

Refund Eligibility

Product/Service Refund Eligibility Conditions
Hardware (New) Yes Unopened, original packaging
Hardware (Used/Refurbished) Yes Defective within warranty period
Software (Unopened) Yes Original seal intact
Software (Opened/Digital) No Non-returnable after license activation
Consultation Services No Service already rendered
Project Deposits Partial Depends on work completed

Refund Process

  1. Request: Submit refund request via email or in writing within eligible period
  2. Review: We will review request within 3 business days
  3. Approval: If approved, we will provide return instructions
  4. Return: Ship item back to us (customer pays return shipping unless defective)
  5. Inspection: We inspect returned items upon receipt
  6. Refund: Process refund within 7 business days after approval

Note: Refunds will be issued to the original payment method. Processing times may vary by financial institution.

Non-Refundable Items

The following are typically non-refundable:

  • Custom configured hardware/software
  • Services already performed
  • Digital downloads after access is provided
  • Gift cards
  • Clearance or "as-is" items

Exceptions

We may make exceptions to this policy in cases of:

  • Our error in order fulfillment
  • Significant delays in delivery
  • Products damaged in transit
  • Special circumstances at our discretion
