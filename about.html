Our Journey

Milestones in our growth and development

2020

Foundation

MUKOTECH NETWORKS was founded by Given Mukwanji with a vision to provide quality IT solutions in Zambia. Started as a small computer repair shop in Kitwe, serving local businesses and individuals with hardware repairs and basic IT support.

2021

First Major Contract

Secured our first corporate client, marking our transition from consumer to business IT services. Expanded our team to include two additional technicians and moved to a larger office space in Ndeke Village.

2022

Service Expansion

Launched our network solutions division and began offering comprehensive IT support packages for small and medium businesses. Became a certified partner for several major technology brands including Microsoft and Cisco.

2023

Digital Transformation

Introduced web development and digital marketing services, completing our transformation into a full-service technology provider. Our team grew to 10 professionals serving clients across the Copperbelt region.

2024

Future Goals

Planning to open a second location in Lusaka and expand our service offerings to include cybersecurity consulting and cloud solutions. Aiming to double our client base and launch an internship program for local IT students.
